# London Bioinformatics Meetup 2026 Context

This note preserves the event-specific context that originally lived in `docs/CLAWBIO-BRIEF.md` when that document functioned as a presentation brief for the project's public launch period.

It is retained as historical reference, not as the current framing for ClawBio.

## Event

- **Event**: London Bioinformatics Meetup — "10 Tips for Becoming a Top 1% AI User"
- **Date**: 26 Feb 2026, 18:45-19:45
- **Venue**: Cavendish Campus, University of Westminster

## How ClawBio Was Framed in the Talk

The talk used a broad AI-fluency structure. Tips 1 through 7 focused on working effectively with AI systems, while tips 8 through 10 pivoted to building with AI. ClawBio was introduced as the central build example: a bioinformatics-native skill library showing how local, reproducible, agent-driven workflows could work in practice.

The live emphasis was on:

- showing ClawBio as something built through practical daily use rather than a grand upfront plan
- positioning the project as a builder's system, not just an analysis demo
- keeping the style grounded and authentic rather than over-produced
- using the Equity Scorer as a concrete, understandable demo for a mixed audience

## Audience

Estimated audience size was roughly 40 to 50 people, including bioinformaticians, computational biologists, data scientists, and non-specialists interested in AI tooling.
