The Idea
What if historical figures had genomes?
Genomebook assigns synthetic diploid genotypes to 20 historical polymaths based on their documented traits, then lets you breed them across generations with real genetic mechanics: Mendelian inheritance, de novo mutations, and clinical evaluation.
Educational. Auditable. Every allele traceable to a trait score and inheritance model.

Skill 1
Soul2DNA Compiler
  • Parse SOUL.md files with 26 trait scores (0.0 to 1.0)
  • Map each trait to loci via trait_registry.json
  • Assign genotypes using additive, dominant, or recessive models
  • Output: diploid .genome.json with 55 loci per soul
  • Sex determination from SOUL.md identity block
# Compile all 20 souls
python skills/soul2dna/soul2dna.py --demo

Skill 2
GenomeMatch Compatibility
Scores all 100 M x F pairings using three metrics, then selects non-overlapping mating pairs via greedy optimisation.
🔀
Heterozygosity 40%
Offspring genetic diversity. More het loci = healthier.
⚖️
Complementarity 40%
Balanced trait combos. Reward differences + high averages.
⚠️
Disease Risk -20%
Flag pairs where both carry recessive disease alleles.

Skill 3
Recombinator Engine
  • Mendelian segregation: one allele from each parent per locus
  • De novo mutation: 0.1% per locus, 3x for cognitive/immune/metabolic hotspots
  • Sex determination: 50/50 XY or XX
  • Trait inference: reverse-map genotype back to scores
  • Clinical eval: disease registry with penetrance and fitness costs
  • Health score: 0.0 to 1.0 from cumulative conditions

Under the Hood
Real Genetics, Synthetic Data
🧬
Inheritance Models
Additive, dominant, and recessive at each locus. Score thresholds determine ref/ref, ref/alt, or alt/alt.
💥
Mutation Engine
De novo mutations classified as disease-risk (1%), protective (0.5%), or neutral (98.5%). Hotspot multipliers for key categories.
🏥
Disease Registry
Autosomal recessive conditions with configurable penetrance, onset probability per generation, and fitness costs.
🔀
Heterosis
Compatibility rewards genetic diversity in offspring. More heterozygous loci = higher score.
📊
Trait Registries
26 traits across 55 loci with chromosomal positions, effect sizes, and pleiotropic handling.
🔍
Full Audit Trail
Every allele is traceable: parent of origin, mutation log, clinical evaluation, and fitness impact.
Try It

# Step 1: Compile 20 historical figures into genomes

# Full pipeline: compile + match + breed generation 1
python skills/soul2dna/soul2dna.py
python skills/genome-match/genome_match.py --generation 0 --top 10
