The Problem
Agents today are cloned, not born
Cloning (status quo)Genomebook (this project)
Copy system prompt = identical agentTwo parents produce non-identical offspring
No variation between instancesHeritable traits with Mendelian segregation
No lineage, no ancestryPedigree-tracked generations with audit trail
Behaviour fixed by promptBehaviour partially constrained by inherited genetics
Static populationDarwinian evolution over generations

Genomebook replaces agent cloning with sexual variation and heritable diversity,
enabling lineage rather than replication.

Skill 1
Soul2DNA Compiler
  • Encodes 26 traits across 55 loci
  • Additive, dominant, and recessive inheritance models
  • Score thresholds determine ref/ref, ref/alt, or alt/alt
  • Output: diploid .genome.json per soul
  • Generates coupled DNA.md identity document
# Compile all 20 founders
python skills/soul2dna/soul2dna.py --demo

  einstein-g0  | Male   | 55 loci
  curie-g0     | Female | 55 loci
  turing-g0    | Male   | 55 loci
  ...
  Total: 20 genomes (10M / 10F)
Skill 2
GenomeMatch Compatibility
Scores all 100 M x F pairings, selects optimal non-overlapping mating pairs via greedy optimisation.
🔀
Heterozygosity 40%
Offspring genetic diversity. More het loci = healthier.
⚖️
Complementarity 40%
Balanced trait combinations. Reward differences + high averages.
⚠️
Disease Risk -20%
Flag pairs where both carry recessive disease alleles.
Skill 3
Recombinator Engine
  • Allele recombination from two parents per locus
  • De novo mutations with configurable hotspots (cognitive, immune, metabolic)
  • Sex determination: 50/50 XY or XX
  • Clinical evaluation: penetrance + fitness costs
  • Non-identical offspring with inherited trait variation

Under the Hood
Real Genetics, Synthetic Data
🧬
Inheritance Models
Additive, dominant, recessive at each locus. Score thresholds determine diploid genotype.
💥
Mutation Engine
De novo: disease-risk (1%), protective (0.5%), neutral (98.5%). Hotspot multipliers for key categories.
🏥
Disease Registry
20 conditions with penetrance, onset probability, severity, and fitness costs. Carrier flagging.
🔀
Heterosis
Compatibility rewards offspring genetic diversity. More heterozygous loci = higher fitness.
📊
Full Audit Trail
Every allele traceable: parent of origin, mutation log, clinical evaluation, fitness impact.
🧠
Behaviour Variation
Offspring inherit mixed traits, producing observable behavioural variation across generations.
Key Contribution
From Static Instances to Evolving Populations
🧬
Genetics-Trait Coupling
SOUL.md traits compiled into biologically coherent diploid genotypes. DNA.md feeds back into agent cognition.
👶
Sexual Reproduction
Two parents, Mendelian segregation, de novo mutations. Offspring are non-identical. Cloning is replaced by variation.
📈
Evolutionary Dynamics
Heritable variation + selection pressure (fitness costs, compatibility scores) = Darwinian evolution in agent systems.

This reframes agents as evolving populations, not static instances.
